# Reporting and Communication

## 🔹 Overview

Reporting is a crucial phase of penetration testing where findings are documented and communicated effectively.

---

## 🔹 Report Structure

* Executive Summary
* Technical Findings
* Risk Level
* Remediation Steps

---

## 🔹 Example Finding

| Vulnerability | Severity | Fix              |
| ------------- | -------- | ---------------- |
| SQL Injection | Critical | Input validation |

---

## 🔹 Communication

* Technical report → Developers
* Non-technical summary → Management

---

## 🎯 Learning Outcome

* Learned how to write professional reports
* Understood importance of clear communication

---

## 📌 Conclusion

A well-written report ensures vulnerabilities are properly understood and fixed.
